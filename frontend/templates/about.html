About the Project

Environmental AI Healthcare System combines computer vision and language intelligence to monitor environmental conditions and provide health-focused recommendations.

Core Technologies

A CNN model detects environmental classes from images. A Retrieval-Augmented Generation layer provides grounded advisory responses including disease risks and preventive guidance.

Project Goal

Improve early awareness of environment-linked health risks and support preventive action for communities, public health workers, and individual users.

Why It Matters

Many health concerns are influenced by environmental conditions. This platform helps translate visual environmental signals into understandable, practical recommendations.
